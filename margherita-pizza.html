Margherita Pizza

Preparation Time

25 minutes

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C.
  2. Spread tomato sauce on dough.
  3. Add cheese and basil.
  4. Bake for 12–15 minutes.
