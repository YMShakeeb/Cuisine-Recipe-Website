Butter Chicken

Preparation Time

45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan and sauté onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned.
  4. Add tomato puree, chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Cook for 10–15 minutes.
  5. Stir in cream and simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.
